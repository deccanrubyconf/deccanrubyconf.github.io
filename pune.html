Weekend in Pune



Weekend in Pune

Planning for a weekend vacation around Pune during the conference?
Here's a handy list of things you can do.

Cafes

Pune is a town for foodies, and we love to spend time enjoying the Irani Cafes, and much more around the city.
Looking for a nice breakfast?
Head over to Vohuman Cafe, Irani Cafe, Cafe Goodluck for a nice cheese omelette with a side of Irani Tea and Bun-Mhaska!
Looking for some Kheema-Pav and sumptious meat delights, Cafe Goodluck, Diamond Queen, and many more.
Looking for some vegetarian options, head over to Vaishali, Wadeshwar, etc.
Shopping

Want to walk around and spend time just shopping, or kill time?
Head over to Phoenix Market City, or the many Central and other malls around.
Hiking and Treks

Want to escape for a quick Hike nearby?
Plan yourself a trek to the many forts around the city!
Punekar's favourite saturday morning trek is Sinhagad, or explore the many other options!
Since its monsoon, you could also head over to Khadakwasla Dam, Bushi Dam, Mulshi Dam, or drive around the waterfalls in the Ghats around Pune!
A good trip is heading to Sinhagad in morning, and coming back down to Khadakwasla on the way and enjoy the Chaupati street food.
Attractions

Want to explore attractions within this historic city?
Visit the Shaniwar Wada fort in the heart of the city.
Other places to visit include Agakhan Palace, which hosts a museum on Mahatma Gandhi and Kasturba Gandhi, Kelkar museum,and more.
While you are at it, visit Pune's magnificient Dagdushet Ganpati, Sarasbaugh and Ganpati Temple, Chaturshring Temple and hike, and many more.
Clubbing

Head over to the many restaurants in Koregaon Park and North main road near the Venue,
with the likes of Hard Rock Cafe, High Spirits, and much more for music lovers!

While you are at it, ping anyone from the staff Vipul, Prahtamesh, Shweta, Anuja, Gautam and Satish for more suggestions or hanging out!
